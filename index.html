Our Story

Jakhura Jewellers began as a family-owned boutique with a simple vision — to craft jewellery that carries meaning, beauty, and cultural significance. What started as a small local store has grown into a trusted brand for jewellery lovers across India. Over the years, we’ve stayed true to our roots while evolving with modern styles and customer needs. Our legacy is built on trust, craftsmanship, and the joy we bring to every customer who wears a piece of Jakhura.

Timeless silver and gold jewellery designed to celebrate every moment of your life.

What We Offer

At Jakhura Jewellers, we offer a beautiful mix of tradition and modern style through our silver and gold jewellery collections. Our silver jewellery, available online, includes everything from elegant earrings and delicate rings to classic bangles and pendants — perfect for everyday wear or special gifts. You can browse and shop our full range anytime, right from the comfort of your home. If you're looking for something truly timeless, our gold jewellery collection is available exclusively in-store. From bridal sets to festive pieces, each item is carefully handcrafted to reflect the beauty of Indian tradition
